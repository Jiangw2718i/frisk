# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report security issues privately. Do **not** open a public issue for a
vulnerability.

Email: security@tryfrisk.dev

Include a description, reproduction steps, and the affected SDK and version. We
aim to acknowledge reports within 48 hours and to provide a remediation timeline
after triage. We will credit reporters who wish to be named once a fix is
released.

## Scope

Frisk performs advisory risk screening. Its lite mode relies on local, public
signals only and is intentionally limited; it is not a guarantee that a
transaction is safe. Treat its output as one signal among several in your
agent's decision process.
